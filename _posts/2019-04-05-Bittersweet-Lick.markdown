Bittersweet Lick  
To my sur-prise,  
the blackbird is  
already spent  

like exhausted Ed, with  
his sore leg—hold-Ed   
him down,   

he said to Will,  
who always Won’t,   
and never looks straight,   

I regretted not   
goIng like Stan,   
to the cold.
